About

It is time to focus on your inner self.

This is a place for you to focus and find inner peace. We will guide you by asking several questions about how you feel and what is in your mind today. This will be a time for you to discover feelings you weren't aware of and embrace them with ZEN (peaceful mind). To get the most out of it, we recommend you to continue every day and see little changes in your mind. Don't forget that it is normal to have emotional ups and downs.
